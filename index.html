01 — The House

Five Points House

A 4,000 sq ft historic estate in the heart of East Nashville — heated saltwater pool, hot tub, private bar, and room for all of us.

6 Bedrooms
5 Bathrooms

The Location

Five Points — East Nashville's most walkable neighborhood. Steps from the best restaurants, bars, and coffee shops in the city. Broadway is a quick ride away.

The Pool + Hot Tub

Heated saltwater pool and hot tub in a private backyard. String lights, lounge chairs, and the kind of setup that makes you never want to leave the house.

The Private Bar

An actual on-site bar. Stock it up, make cocktails, and have a proper night in without going anywhere. This house was literally made for groups like us.

The Vibe

Dark moody interiors, modern finishes, and that perfect mix of historic charm and cool design. It photographs beautifully — trust.

03 — What to Wear

Style Guide

No theme. No matching shirts. No cowgirl costumes. The only dress code is: look as hot as humanly possible. Bring your best fits and stay on trend.

Going Out

Your hottest going-out look. A satin mini, a slip dress, a mesh moment — whatever makes you feel like that girl. Dark tones, metallics, bold color, all fair game. Heeled boots or strappy sandals. Big earrings.

Slip dress Satin Mesh Metallics Heeled boots
Daytime

Hot but comfortable. Linen sets, a great sundress, or denim shorts done right. You will be photographed — dress accordingly.

Linen sets Sundress Denim
Shoes

Bring boots if you want — but make them sleek. Black, tan, or white leather. Also bring heels for dinner and something cute but walkable for daytime. Your feet will thank you.

Sleek boots Heels Cute flats
Accessories

Layered gold, oversized sunglasses, a small bag so your hands are free for drinks. Go heavy on the jewelry — this is not a minimalist weekend.

Gold layers Oversized sunnies Small bag
Pool

The pool is stunning and there will be photos. Bring a swimsuit you actually love and a coverup that could double as an outfit. Cute slides. That's it.

Hot swimsuit Coverup Slides
The Group Shot

For the inevitable group photo: loosely coordinate, don't match. Pick from the palette below so we look intentional without looking like a bridal party at a theme park.
